About Me

Getting to know the person behind the portfolio

Hello! I am Alexandra Louise G. Guillen

I am from Butuan City, and I currently study at the University of San Carlos. As a first-year student in Information Technology, I'm on an exciting journey to explore the vast world of computing and technology.

My interest in technology began with curiosity about how things work behind the screens we interact with daily. From simple websites to complex applications, I wanted to understand the logic and creativity that powers our digital world.

This portfolio represents my first semester learning journey in Introduction to Computing. Each portfolio reflects not just academic learning, but also my personal growth, challenges overcome, and moments of discovery as I navigate this new field.

When I'm not studying, I enjoy exploring creative design, reading books, and spending time with my pets.
